This R package, SpatPred, includes all scripts and functions needed to conduct 
analyses reported in the article "On extrapolating past the range of observed 
data when making statistical predictions in ecology" by Paul B. Conn, Devin S. 
Johnson, and Peter L. Boveng

Scripts to conduct simulations and analysis of ribbon seal data are located in 
the ./SpatPred/inst directory, while base functions are in the ./SpatPred/R 
directory.  Ribbon seal data are accessible by typing "data(Ribbon_data)"
